Biography

Hi, I'm Yuliy, I write bizarre horror stories for cultured weirdos like me. Do you like heads exploding into guts and eyeballs? No? Well here you go!

I generally work digitally as it's faster and more productive, though I have experience working analogue as well. I keep my pages to black and white, but I have plenty of coloured works too.

I'm currently working on my second book; a collection of short horror stories. With any luck it's set to be out by Christmas!

Journey to Andromeda

In this surreal story, creepy monsters are the least of your worries. From the comforts of earth to the cold reaches of space, we'll travel through the universe like you've never seen before! Yuliy presents Journey to Andromeda, a dark tale you won't ever forget.
